Our Services

Aerial Video & Photographs

From action cams to cinematic & drone based cameras; Bitsy has the video/photo equipment necessary to get interesting and beautiful footage for any of our customer's needs.

Video Editing (Aerial/Ground)

Bitsy has a skilled team with plenty of professional video production under their belts. Whatever your needs may be; Bitsy is capable of executing anything you have dreamt up. Or they will dream it up for you.

Survey & Inspection Services

Bitsy is capable of surveying & inspecting hard to access locations with the help of our drones. From inspecting buildings and properties to surveying large pieces of property, Bitsy is capable of supporting you.

Asset Inspection

Reduce the cost of inspections through the use of Bitsy's drones. We will enable you to make quick and efficient first pass inspections to identify issues. We provide on-site live video feeds, recorded HD, and we are able to stream drone video feeds in real time.

Construction Reporting

Track project milestones, contractor progress, and the overall picture of a construction project over time through the use of our aerial video & photo capabilities. We are able to provide weekly/monthly construction reports using photographs or video.

Aerial Survey + 3D Mapping

Bitsy is capable of aerial surveys with GPS coordinates via drone based photo and/or video. Want to take it further with a 3D map of the area? Not a problem, we offer 3D mapping & volumetric calculations using our drone's on board hardware and photogrammetry software.

Meet the Team

Doug Depker

CO-OWNER & OPERATIONS

Doug is a long time drone and video editting enthusiast. He began making videos of friend/family trips and eventually got in to editting marketing videos professionally. He has been building & flying drones for many years, so Bitsy became a natural fit with the recent wave of drone platforms and technology. Doug attended Arizona State Univeristy for a bachelors in Business Admin. He worked in the supply chain industry for 9 years out of college, but eventually found himself longing to work in a field he was passionate about , which led him to drone and video production services via Bitsy Drone Services.

Joe Hoffmans

CO-OWNER & BUSSINES DEVELOPMENT

Joe graduated from the University of Houston with a degree in Business Management. He has been working in the Logistics/Freight industry for the last 8 years in supervisory and sales roles. Joe has been fascinated by drones over the last 5 years and wanted to be a part of how they will change the future of a lot of industries/services. He has spent time creating videos and performing services using drones which led him to help create Bitsy Drone Services.
